How to Pay Taxes

Pay your utilities, garbage disposal fees, and property taxes using the following methods:

Convenient Methods of Payment
Online Banking Pay via your financial institution's online web banking or telephone banking.
In Person Pay in person on the first floor of City Hall during regular office hours. An after hours letter drop box is located at both entrance doors of City Hall. These drop boxes are cleared daily and also at midnight on the Utilities and Tax due date.
Mail Mail your cheque and stub well in advance to the Burnaby Tax Office, Box 6200 Van Stn Main, V6B 4B5.
ATM Pay your bill through the Automated Bank Machine.
Financial Institution Pay via tellers at most banks and credit unions.
PAD Pay through automated monthly withdrawal from your account to ensure timely payments.

Important Note

These methods are subject to penalties if rejected by your financial institution or if you pay after your financial institution's local cut off time on the due date. These payments will be processed with a "payment date" equal to the next payment date.
